🛠️ Enhancing Your Home Oven for Pizza

Baking steel or thick metal plate — Highly recommended. A 6–10 mm thick steel plate on the lowest rack or oven floor stores massive heat and delivers it directly to the base of the pizza. Result: faster bake, crispier bottom, better leopard spotting.

How to use it safely:

  • Preheat oven + steel for 45–60 minutes at your highest safe temperature (230–250°C).
  • Use a pizza peel with semolina or flour for easy launch.
  • Never put cold/wet items directly on a screaming hot steel.
  • Let it cool slowly in the oven after use. Avoid thermal shock.

Pizza stone works similarly but can crack more easily from moisture or sudden temperature changes. Steel is generally more forgiving for home use.

Other useful tools: perforated pizza pan, good wooden or metal peel, and a bench scraper for handling dough.
